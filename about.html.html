About Us

Optimum Family, Mother & Child Nutrition.

OUR VISION.

A world where all families have access to optimum nutrition for every mother and child.

OUR GOALS

Promote diets & dietary services for growth and develoment of all children
and a health productive adult life of every family member.

OUR OBJECTIVES

Prevent macro and micro nutrients deficiences and obesity in early childhood
provide healthy diet and promote healthy dietary services to all mothers of reproductive age.
Prevent dietary related complications during pregnancy and post delivery amoung all mothers.
Scale down on food wastage and increase local farmers household income.
